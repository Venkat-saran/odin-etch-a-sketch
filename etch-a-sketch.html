  • Hold down 'Shift' and hover over existing Trail to erase it. (like an Eraser tool)
  • Hold down the 'Ctrl' key to hover safely without leaving a Trail.
  • Press 'q' or 'Q' to clear the board.
  • Setting the value to 'persist' will leave a trail, like a pen on a board would.
  • 'Don't Persist' will make the cursor feel like a pointer of the current color, moving across the board as the cursor moves.
